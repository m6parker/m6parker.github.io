Apps

  • 16oz package pasta
  • 1tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1can condensed soup
  • 15oz can mixed vegetables
  • 2cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 15oz can tuna, drained
  • salt and pepper

step 1

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add 1 tbsp of veg oil. Add pasta and follow the box instructions for cook time. Drain pasta and preheat oven to 350degrees.

step 2

In a 2 quart saucepan over medium heat combine condensed soup, vegetables canned tuna, and half the cheddar cheese. fold pasta into soup mixture and add to baking dish. cover mixture with the rest of the cheese

step 3

bake until cheese is melted.
